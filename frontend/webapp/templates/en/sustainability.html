Sustainability
 
Sustainability at JBS is a transversal commitment of the Business Units with the Company sectors in all countries where the company operates and runs the business. It is all based on ethical and transparent performance, a constructive relationship with stakeholders, and responsibility for treating people, animals, and the environment.
One of the most important actions is the program Juntos pela Amazônia (Together for the Amazon), which is part of this engagement and encourages the development of the Amazon biome. It promotes the conservation and sustainable use of the forest, improves the quality of life of the local population, and advances new technologies to preserve the environment.
